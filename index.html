Introduction

Kickstart Your Career With BBA Course in Nepal from Kantipur City College (KCC). Our BBA program focuses on a strong ethos of openness, sharing, and commitment, instilling knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in the dynamic global environment.

Discover a unique 4-year journey that combines mandatory courses and specialization. Choose from Marketing, HRM, Finance, International Business, and Accounting. Our curriculum, spanning 120 credits, ensures a holistic education.

Objectives

The course aims to:

  • Acquire a solid foundation in core business disciplines such as accounting, finance, marketing, management, and operations.
  • Develop practical skills and competencies in areas such as data analysis, communication, problem-solving, and teamwork.
  • Gain a global perspective on business and understand the impact of international trade, cultural differences, and economic policies on business operations.
  • Build a strong professional network through internships, industry partnerships, and networking events.
  • Develop leadership and management skills that are essential for career advancement.
  • Prepare for graduate-level education in business, such as an MBA or a specialized master’s degree.
